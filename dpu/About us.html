Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune (Deemed to be University) Established on January 11, 2003, by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956, Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune, is a distinguished institution with 14 constituent units. These units have collectively built the brand "DPU," offering programs in Medicine, Dentistry, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Optometry, Biotechnology, Management, Ayurved, Homeopathy, Design, Allied Health Sciences, Liberal Arts, Science & Technology, and Online Learning. The university is recognized for its state-of-the-art infrastructure and dedicated faculty. ,

DPU has garnered several prestigious accolades, including the QS I-GAUGE Diamond Rating and ranks between 601-800 in the Times Higher Education Impact Rankings 2024. It has been accredited by NAAC with an 'A++' grade, ISO certifications, and recognized as a Category-I University by the UGC.

Notably, DPU ranks 5th in the Dental category, 11th in Medical, and 44th in the University category under the NIRF 2024. The university also ranks 9th among the cleanest residential universities in India under the Swachh Campus Rankings 2019.

In addition to academic excellence, DPU is a recognized Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and has accredited hospitals and laboratories by NABH and NABL. The university also hosts a GMP-certified Regenerative Medicine Laboratory and has received DST-FIST funding for its Biotechnology and Bioinformatics Institute.

DPU's medical college is a recognized regional center for conducting workshops for PG teachers, and its journals are indexed in SCOPUS and UGC-CARE.
